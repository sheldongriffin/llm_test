WHAT "INSTALL & GO" GIVES YOU
No layer AI directly connected to email + data lake with no gateway, no DLP, no content filter, no audit trail, no policy, and no one trained to know what any of that means.
When something goes wrong — and it will — you won't know what happened, when, or who caused it. Logging after the fact is forensics. This architecture is prevention.

On "AI Firewall" Products Tools like Lakera Guard, Aporia, or Azure APIM with custom policies can handle many gateway functions in one product — prompt injection, content safety, DLP, logging. None of them replace identity, governance, or training. They are the middle layer, not the whole stack.
The Agentic Risk That's Usually Missed When an AI agent generates code or takes actions, it makes hundreds of implicit policy decisions (error handling, auth fallback, retry logic) that no human ever consciously approved. "CLAUDE.md" helps but doesn't solve this. Human review checkpoints are non-optional for any write-capable agent.
Where to Start If You're Behind 1. Audit what's already connected (email, data lake — right now). 2. Block new AI tool procurement without review. 3. Read-only service accounts for all data connectors. 4. Logging pipeline before you expand access further. 5. Then gateway. Everything else follows.
